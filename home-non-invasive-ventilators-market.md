<p><strong>The Home Non invasive Ventilators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Level Ventilators</li><li>Bilevel Ventilators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The home non-invasive ventilators market is primarily segmented into two types: single-level and bilevel ventilators. Single-level ventilators provide a constant airflow, ideal for patients with stable breathing patterns, typically used in conditions like obstructive sleep apnea. In contrast, bilevel ventilators offer two distinct pressure settings: one for inhalation and another for exhalation, catering to patients with fluctuating respiratory needs, such as those with chronic obstructive pulmonary disease. Both types enhance patient comfort and support respiratory function at home.</p></p>
