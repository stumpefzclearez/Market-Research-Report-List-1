<p><p>The outdoor fire protection coatings market is primarily segmented into water-based and solvent-based types. Water-based coatings are environmentally friendly, offering easy application and quick drying times, making them suitable for a variety of surfaces. They provide effective fire resistance while minimizing harmful emissions. In contrast, solvent-based coatings typically offer enhanced durability and resistance to harsh weather, thus suitable for extreme conditions. These formulations usually have longer drying times but are commonly preferred for their robust protective properties in demanding environments.</p></p>
