<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar-powered pump market features two main types: surface suction pumps and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps operate from above ground, drawing water from shallow sources like ponds or tanks. They are ideal for irrigation and livestock watering. In contrast, submersible pumps are designed to be submerged in water, efficiently lifting water from deep wells or boreholes. Both types leverage solar energy, providing eco-friendly, cost-effective solutions for water management in agriculture, residential, and industrial applications.</p></p>
