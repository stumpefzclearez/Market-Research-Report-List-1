<p><strong>The Strerilizer Steam Generators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Boiler</li><li>Remote Boiler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilizer steam generators market is primarily divided into two types: integral boilers and remote boilers. Integral boilers are compact units integrated within the sterilization equipment, providing efficient steam generation directly at the point of use. In contrast, remote boilers function independently from the sterilizers, allowing for centralized steam generation that can serve multiple sterilizers or other equipment. Both types cater to different operational needs, with integral boilers offering convenience and space-saving, while remote boilers provide scalability and flexibility for larger facilities.</p></p>
