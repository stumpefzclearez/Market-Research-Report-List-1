<p><p>The mineral fibre market comprises various types, including Glasswool, Rockwool, Slagwool, Glass Filaments, and Ceramic Fibres. Glasswool is lightweight and offers excellent thermal insulation, while Rockwool, derived from basalt, provides enhanced fire resistance and sound insulation. Slagwool, made from steel industry by-products, is cost-effective and suitable for insulation applications. Glass Filaments are used in reinforced composites for strength, and Ceramic Fibres withstand high temperatures, making them ideal for industrial insulation. Each type serves distinct applications across construction and manufacturing sectors.</p></p>
