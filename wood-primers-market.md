<p><strong>The Wood Primers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil-Based Primer</li><li>Water-Based Primer</li><li>Shellac-Based Primer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wood primers market comprises three main types: oil-based, water-based, and shellac-based primers. Oil-based primers offer excellent adhesion and stain-blocking properties, making them ideal for exterior use and hard-to-cover stains. Water-based primers are low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and dry quickly, providing easier cleanup and environmental benefits. Shellac-based primers excel at sealing knots and preventing resin bleed, making them suitable for tricky wood types. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to various project requirements and preferences.</p></p>
