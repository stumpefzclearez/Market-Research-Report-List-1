<p><p>Polymer-coated Sulfur-coated Urea (PCSCU) fertilizers are advanced controlled-release fertilizers designed to optimize nutrient delivery to crops. These fertilizers consist of urea granules coated with a layer of sulfur and polymer, which regulates the release of nitrogen into the soil over an extended period. This technology minimizes nutrient leaching, enhances soil microbial activity, and improves crop yields by providing a steady supply of nitrogen.</p><p>The PCSCU fertilizers market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-efficiency fertilizers to support sustainable agriculture practices. Farmers are increasingly adopting these fertilizers for their ability to improve nutrient use efficiency and reduce environmental impact. The market is also benefiting from rising awareness about the benefits of controlled-release fertilizers among farmers and stakeholders in the agricultural sector.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in agricultural technology and the growing trend toward precision farming are contributing to the market's expansion.
