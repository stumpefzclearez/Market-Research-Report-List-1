<p><p>The Pressure Infusion Set market comprises two primary types: Manual Pressure Infusion Sets and Automatic Pressure Infusion Sets. Manual sets require healthcare professionals to physically exert pressure on the infusion bag to control fluid delivery, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. In contrast, Automatic Pressure Infusion Sets utilize mechanical pumps to maintain consistent pressure, enhancing precision and reducing manual labor. Both types are essential in emergency and critical care settings, ensuring rapid fluid administration during patient treatment.</p></p>
