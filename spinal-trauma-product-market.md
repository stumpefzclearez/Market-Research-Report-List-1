<p><p>The spinal trauma product market is divided into two primary categories: spinal fusion and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products involve surgical techniques that permanently connect two or more vertebrae, stabilizing the spine and alleviating pain caused by injuries or degeneration. Non-fusion products focus on devices that support spinal stability without permanently fusing vertebrae, allowing for more mobility and natural movement. Both types aim to address various spinal injuries and conditions, enhancing patient recovery and quality of life.</p></p>
