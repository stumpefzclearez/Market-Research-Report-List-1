<p><p>The automobile brake components market includes various essential parts that ensure effective vehicle braking. Brake pads are friction materials that clamp onto rotors, providing stopping force. Brake shoes serve a similar purpose in drum systems. Brake lining consists of friction materials found in both shoe and pad designs. Brake rotors are metal discs that work with pads to halt wheel motion, while brake drums enclose shoes in a drum brake system. Other components include hardware and sensors that enhance braking efficiency.</p></p>
