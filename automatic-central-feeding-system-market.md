<p><p>The Automatic Central Feeding System market comprises two primary transport types: dilute phase and dense phase transport. Dilute phase transport involves the movement of materials at high velocity and low pressure, allowing for efficient transfer over long distances, typically suited for lighter materials. In contrast, dense phase transport operates at lower velocities and higher pressures, enabling the movement of heavier materials with reduced particle degradation, making it ideal for fragile or abrasive substances. Both systems enhance productivity in various industrial applications.</p></p>
